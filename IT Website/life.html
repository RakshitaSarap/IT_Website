Spandan - Our Annnual Cultural Fest

"Spandan" is not just an event; it is a celebration of diversity, creativity, and the unifying power of culture. Our institution takes immense pride in organizing “Spandan”, and as the Cultural Secretary, I am thrilled to present to you a glimpse of the incredible talents and passion that our cultural contingent embodies.



Transmission - Our Annual Technical Fest

Transmission is the annual inter-college Tech Festival at XIE.Transmission is a major festival on the college tech-fest circuit in Mumbai, with several repeat entries year after year.



Guest Lectures

Various Guest Lectures for IT students were conducted to help them showcase their technical prowess and creativity in an engaging in, unveiling innovative solutions and demonstrating their excellence in the field of technology.



Mini Project Competition

The IT Department recently concluded its highly anticipated Mini Project Competition, with resounding success. This event showcased the remarkable talents and innovative spirit of our students, creating a dynamic platform for them to excel in the realm of Information Technology
